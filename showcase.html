The Heart of the Game

Trust & Discovery

♥ ♥ ♥ ♥ ♥ ♥ ♥ 4 / 7 trust

"You don't catch horses. You earn them."

A wild horse appears because you grew something it loves. Feed it the right flower and trust inches up — but only once per visit. Rush it and the horse spooks.


Common horses take a handful of feedings. The rarest take many sessions. This is the heart of the game: a relationship on the horse's terms.

Your Growing Farm

Start Small. Expand Forever.

Begin with one small garden. Earn coins, buy more plots, expand your fields. Each plot can hold up to 25 gardens.

Tap a plot to zoom in and tend it. Zoom out to see your whole farm at a glance — a patchwork of blooming gardens.

The Infinity Loop

Every Horse Makes You Stronger

Tame a horse and its passive perk kicks in. Keep feeding it and the perk levels up. Stack enough horses and your farm practically tends itself.

"Every horse makes your farm better.
Every farm attracts rarer horses."
